What's this all about?

Coming together to understand how today's technology-infused world affects how we think and live - and how we can adapt. This is a place to help each other find a balance between human nature and human progress.

Why now?

Because today's environment is changing faster than ever before and it is influencing the way we think and act. Who we are tomorrow depends on the information and experience we consume today and, whether we realize it or not, we're all training for something..
